Welcome

Dr. Dean Dorman is a licensed pscychologist who holds a doctorate from Western Michigan University. With over twenty years of experience, Dr. Dorman currently works as a therapist in private practice where he specializes in couples counseling.

He is considered an expert in the areas of depression, anger management, and adult Attention Deficit Disorder.

His current practice is located at Creative Solutions Counseling located at 2305 East Paris Ave. Grand Rapids, Michigan. For more information, or to schedule an appointment, please call 616-929-0226.
