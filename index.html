About Dr. Dorman

Dr. Dean Dorman is a licensed psychologist who holds a doctorate from Western Michigan University. With over twenty years of experience, Dr. Dorman currently works as a therapist in private practice where he specializes in couples counseling.

He is an expert in the areas of depression, anger management, and adult Attention Deficit Disorder.

His current practice is located at Creative Solutions Counseling located at 2305 East Paris Ave. Grand Rapids, Michigan. For more information, or to schedule an appointment, please call 616-929-0226.

Second Chance at a Great Relationship

Noted psychologist Dr. Dean Dorman has spent over 20 years saving marriages. What he was learned in that time is that most marriages start out happy but as resentments grow, couples start to disconnect emotionally. This book guides you through the process of understanding how to settle unresolved issues within your relationship. Through a series of self-tests (Dorman Resentment Rating Scale and Barriers to Resolving Conflicts Questionnaire) you can understand the source of your resentment toward your mate and, more importantly, identify the barriers that keep you from resolving your issues.
