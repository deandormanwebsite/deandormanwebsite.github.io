About Dr. Dorman

I am a licensed psychologist and hold a doctorate from Western Michigan University. I have been a therapist in private practice specializing in couples counseling for over twenty five years. Currently, I reside with my family in West Michigan. I am considered an expert in the areas of couples counseling, depression, anger management and Attention Deficit Disorder. I enjoy reading, it is one of my passions. I have a wide range of interests from Stephen King to Preston and Childs and I occasionally allow myself a fantasy trilogy. My other interests are travel (mostly to warmer climates since I live in Michigan), golf and exercising. I am also the author of the book Second Chance at A Great Relationship, (Principia Media, 2012).
